My Top Five Favorite Fortnite Seasons

  1. Chapter 3 Season 2

      From the heights of the Fortnite storyline, new gameplay features such as the ability to drive tanks, and amazing gunplay this season had it all! Constant weekly updates and changes to the map in addition to progression of Fortnite's storyline every week made this season an incredibly fun one to play all leading up to what is probably my favorite live event off all time. All of this and more lead this season to be my personal pick for my favorite Fortnite season of all time.

  2. Chapter 2 Season 7

      3. An awesome alien based theme with special themed weapons as well as other themed locations and items lead this season to take my second pick. In addition to this it had a cool live event and a great loot pool with a lot of weapons I loved brought back from past seasons.

  3. Chapter 6 Season 2

      4. This season was pure chaos but, in the best way. Themed on criminals and heists there were plenty of new specially themed items and challenges to do which made this season a blast to play. This and a whole lot more is the reason why its my third favorite Fortnite Season of all time.

  4. Chapter 2 Season 2

      5. With a awesome spy based theme, with special themed weapons, and a really good end of season live event this season was a great one to play through at the time. It was fun with friends and fun playing solo. This is why personally, it is my fourth favorite season of Fortnite.

  5. Chapter 1 Season 7

      6. With its fun Christmas themed map and items as well as new vehicles like the X-4 Stormwing and Driftboard make this season one of my favorites. Additionally, this season is important because it added the Creative mode which would change the game forever allowing players to make there own games inside of Fortnite.
