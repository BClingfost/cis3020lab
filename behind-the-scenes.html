Fortnite Fun Facts

  1. Origionaly Fortnite started as a player versus computer zombie game taking place in a world were 98% of the human population had disappeared.
  2. Fortnite has multiple game modes made by the developers at Epic Games, all using the same engine including vasltly different modes such as the Lego mode.
  3. The first teaser for Fortnite was released nearly 15 years ago in December of 2011 with this teaser being about the previously mentioned origional zombie game.
