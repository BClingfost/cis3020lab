How did I become a fan?

I first became a fan of the game during its season 2 update in december 2017. I became a fan mainly due to a combination of youtubers, friends my own age playing the game at the time, and me just finding the game enjoyable. I have remained a fan of the game too this day.

What do I love most about the game?

My favorite part of the game is playing with my friends.

What would I recommend to someone just getting started?

If someone was just starting out I would recomend to not quit the game to early and to try out all the different gamemodes the game has too offer (both developer made and player made). Every player is different so you may find a mode you like that isn't the main battle royale mode.

Do I have any favorite fan communities or creators?

One of my favorite Fortnite creators is the youtuber Fe4rless. Not only are his videos very funny (in my opinion) but his video editing skills and skills at the game are both very good aswell.
