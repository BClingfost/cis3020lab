How did I become a fan?

I first became a fan of the game during its season 2 update in December 2017. I became a fan mainly due to a combination of youtubers, friends my own age playing the game, and just finding the game enjoyable after trying it. I am still currently a fan of the game.

What do I love most about the game?

My favorite part of the game is playing it with my friends.

What would I recommend to someone just getting started?

If someone was just starting I would recomend to stick the game out and to try all the different gamemodes it has too offer (both developer made and player made). If you do not enjoy the main battle royale mode there are many others to play. So, you may find a mode you like that isn't the main one.

Do I have any favorite fan communities or creators?

One of my favorite Fortnite creators is the youtuber Fe4rless. Not only are his videos very funny (in my opinion) but his video editing skills and skills at the game in general are both very good aswell.
